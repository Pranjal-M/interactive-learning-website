The second largest planet in our solar system, adorned with thousands of beautiful ringlets. It is not the only planet to have rings, but none are as spectacular or as complicated as Saturn's. Saturn is a massive ball of mostly hydrogen and helium. 

Surrounded by 53 moons, Saturn is home to some of the most fascinating landscapes in our solar system. The Saturn system is a rich source of scientific discovery and still holds many mysteries. 
<br><br>Saturn is named after the Roman god of agriculture and wealth.<br><br><br>
